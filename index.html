📏 What is "Runway"?

Runway is a quick, rough estimate of how many years your total assets (Cash + ISA + Pension) would last if you spent your Phase 1 amount every year, with no investment growth and no inflation factored in.

It's simply: Total Assets ÷ Phase 1 Spend.

It's a sanity-check number, not a forecast — your real outcome depends on growth, inflation and tax, which is what the full Table and Charts tabs calculate properly year by year. Runway turns green if it covers your full spending plan, amber if it's close, and red if it falls well short.
